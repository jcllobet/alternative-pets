# Alternative Pets

Alternative Pets is a community collection of expressive, original companions for Codex. The goal is to make it easy to create, improve, and share pets that feel personal while meeting Codex's animation and quality requirements.

Contributions are welcome: new pets, repaired animations, stronger visual consistency, QA improvements, and documentation. Keep submissions original and rights-safe, and include the installable pet plus enough QA evidence for reviewers to trust it.

Start with [prompt.md](prompt.md) and the [OpenAI Hatch Pet skill](https://github.com/openai/skills/tree/main/skills/.curated/hatch-pet), then open a pull request with your pet in its own folder. We would love to see what you make.
