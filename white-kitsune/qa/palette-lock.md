# White Kitsune palette lock

The canonical base defines the authoritative flat colors for every generated frame:

- fur white: `#FDFDFC`
- vermilion markings, paws, and single tail tip: `#F72A4F`
- deep indigo outline and facial linework: `#050C67`
- soft fur shadow: `#DCCAD8`

Antialiasing may introduce only tiny edge blends around these colors. Do not shift the fur toward gray, cream, blue, or pink. Do not shift the red accent toward orange, magenta, burgundy, or a different saturation. The same palette must remain visibly stable within every row and across all rows.
